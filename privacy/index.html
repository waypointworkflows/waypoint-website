Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

Waypoint Workflows, LLC (“Waypoint,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through waypointworkflows.com, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

When you submit a contact or service request, we may collect your name, business name, email address, phone number, business type, current website, preferred contact method, and the information you provide about your business needs.

We may also receive limited technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and general usage data through hosting, security, or analytics services.

How we use information

  • Respond to inquiries and evaluate requested services.
  • Communicate about Waypoint services and active work.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the website and related systems.
  • Meet legal, accounting, or compliance obligations.

How information is shared

Waypoint does not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that support website hosting, communications, workflow processing, security, or business operations. Those providers receive only the information reasonably necessary to perform their services.

Data retention and security

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, ongoing business relationships, legal obligations, and legitimate recordkeeping. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of information you submitted, subject to applicable legal and operational requirements. You may also ask us to stop non-essential communications.

External links

This website may link to third-party websites. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Children

This website is intended for business users and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as our services or legal requirements change. The effective date above identifies the current version.

Contact

Questions or requests may be sent to bill@waypointworkflows.com.

Legal review note: This launch policy is a practical business template and should be reviewed by Arkansas counsel before Waypoint begins collecting sensitive information, using behavioral advertising, or serving regulated industries.
