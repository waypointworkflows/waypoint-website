Digital Front Desk

Every customer request captured. Every next action visible.

The Digital Front Desk turns scattered calls, forms, messages, and referrals into a structured operating queue.

Map my front desk
From inquiry to action

Replace the pile of messages with a working process.

Capture the request

Website form, call, referral, email, or staff entry becomes one consistent record.

Understand what the customer needs

Service type, urgency, location, project context, and missing information are organized.

Assign the next action

The right person is notified with a clear task—not a forwarded message and a guess.

Keep the request visible

Open leads, waiting customers, quotes, and scheduled work remain visible until resolved.

Built around the owner’s questions.

  • Who needs a response?
  • Which requests are real opportunities?
  • What information is still missing?
  • What has been quoted?
  • What is waiting on the customer?
  • What needs attention today?
Typical information captured

Useful facts, not a generic contact card.

Customer, contact method, request summary, service type, urgency, quote readiness, clarification questions, assigned person, lead status, next action, and follow-up date.

Owner Dashboard

The visibility layer is developing alongside the workflow.

Early implementations can include practical reporting and notifications now. A focused Owner Dashboard is developing to consolidate Today’s Actions, Open Leads, Quote Tracker, Scheduled Work, and attention flags into one mobile-friendly view.

Developing with early customers
