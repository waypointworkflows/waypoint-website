Business Presence

A website that does more than prove you exist.

Business Presence gives a small business a clear, credible customer destination—and turns interest into a structured next step.

Build my business presence

Built for the questions real customers ask.

What do you do? Do you serve my area? Can I trust you? How do I reach you? What happens after I contact you?

A Waypoint website answers those questions quickly, works on a phone, and gives each visitor a sensible route forward.

Every Business Presence includes:

  • Mobile-first, professional design
  • Clear service and customer-path content
  • Contact and request forms structured for follow-up
  • Search and social metadata
  • Accessibility and performance fundamentals
  • Cloudflare deployment readiness
Four business models

Designed around how the customer chooses.

Service

For trades, field services, repair, maintenance, property services, and local operators.

Appointment

For professional offices, healthcare, personal services, and scheduled consultations.

Showcase

For businesses where customers need to see products, environments, or finished work.

Portfolio

For specialists whose past projects, expertise, and approach drive the buying decision.
